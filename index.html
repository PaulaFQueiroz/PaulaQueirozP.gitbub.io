Empowering Businesses with Informed Decision-Making

As a data expert, I specialize in transforming raw data into compelling narratives to drive informed decisions and continuous improvements.

I am a versatile, results-driven professional with a strong foundation in statistics and decision sciences and a keen interest that lie in the intersection of fields such as technology, cognitive sciences, operations management, policy, and governance. After graduating Magna Cum Laude from San Francisco State University with a B.S. in Statistics, I have honed my analytical skills and developed a robust understanding of how to utilize statistical and computational methods to solve business problems. My professional experience encompasses a diverse array of projects in data analysis, process monitoring, and quality assurance for high-profile clients, including the Internal Revenue Service (IRS), Social Security Administration (SSA), and The National Gallery of Art. With a proven track record of applying data-driven insights to real-world challenges, I am dedicated to contributing to projects that deliver optimal, responsible, and sustainable solutions in business and governance.
